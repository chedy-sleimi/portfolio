# ADR-0004: Facts source of truth; Neotest shown in words only

Date: 2026-07-21 · Status: accepted

## Context

The site's credibility strategy is honesty (visa status stated plainly, junior level
owned). Neotest is Neoalians intellectual property, built during an internship; no
permission has been sought to show product UI or code.

## Decision

- Every fact on the site traces to the owner's master profile (career-ops
  `about-me.md`, itself fed by `cv.md`). No invented claims, metrics, or attributions.
- The Neotest case study uses words and an owner-drawn pipeline representation only:
  no product screenshots, no source, no repo link, and it states that plainly.
- Work authorization (non-EU, sponsorship required, available September 2026) is
  stated in About/Contact and the footer — findable in one skim, not in the hero.

## Consequences

If Neoalians later approves visuals, the case study can gain screenshots without
restructuring. Any new claim requires updating the master profile first.
